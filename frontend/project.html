    This is our very first project as an organization, This a seasonal project with a valentine theme in mind, created with the goal of helping spread love, care and affection in the spirit of valentine to the less priviledge individuals in the society. In a season where love is in the air and eveyone is being merry, we developed this project to channel such love into giving, not just to our friends, families, well wishers but instead to someone we don't know like the scrunny begger across the street, the orphans and many more. This project is aimed at spreading love and care in the spirit of valentine by working togther with people, inspired by the need to spread love to certain individuals who need it and have no one to give them such love. This project creates a platform for people all over the world to send out their love and care to anyone which could be your friends, family, well wishers and most importantly to people who really need such love.
